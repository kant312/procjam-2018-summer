Procedural Painter

Made by @kant312 for Procjam 2018

Procedural Painter is a "procedural thingy" where you paint by being only partially in control.

  • You decide on the amount of brushes, their shape (squares or circles) and their orientation (which only makes sense for squares of course).
  • You can set the initial size of the brushes, but they will change constantly.
  • You can change the color of the brushes but the color is chosen randomly and keeps evolving all the time, for each brush.
  • You decide if the shape has a border or not.

When you are ready to start painting, press "p" or click on "Toggle paint mode", this will stop the screen from being refreshed and the brushes will start painting.

You can click on "Export painting" when you are satisfied with the painting.
